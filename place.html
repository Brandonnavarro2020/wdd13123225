About Madrid

Madrid, Cundinamarca, is a vibrant municipality known for its fruit markets, nearby natural reserves and friendly local culture. This guide gives practical tips and highlights for visitors.

Quick tips

  • Best time to visit: January–March and July–August (dry months).
  • Transport: local buses to surrounding veredas; taxis from the central market.
  • Local customs: greet with a handshake; friendly and informal conversations are common.
